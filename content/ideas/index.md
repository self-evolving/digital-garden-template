---
title: "Ideas"
description: "Atomic idea notes, from fresh seedlings to evergreen notes."
---

One idea per note. Each note carries a `status` that signals how much you can
lean on it:

- `seedling` — freshly planted, unpolished, may be wrong
- `budding` — has been tended; the shape is there, edges still soft
- `evergreen` — distilled and stable; safe to build on

Statuses are promoted (or demoted) during tending, not at capture time.
